Who we are

Swiss Chocolate was established in the Cotswold village of Broadway, in 18881 by Andrew and Sheila Campbell.

Our Chocolate

The desire was to put the fun back into buying sweets, by creating an old fashioned sweet shop. The shop would specialise in selling traditional sweets toffees and fudges out of old fashioned glass jars.

Events

In addiition to our finely-crafted handmade chocolates, we also sell our products at independent retailers, cater for wedding and corporate favours or requests for special events.

About us

The shop was an instant success and realising how important the chocolates had become to the business Andrew and Sheila decided to open their own factory and develop their own exclusive recipes.

What Makes Great Handmade Chocolate?

A good quality chocolate should titillate all your senses; it is the overall balance of the different facets of a chocolate that will make it an enjoyable experience. Have a go at chocolate tasting.

The Five Senses

Sight, Smell, Touch, Hearing and, of course, Taste. When it comes to great handmade chocolate, we ensure that no sense is excluded. Good quality handmade chocolate should titillate all your senses.

What to look for

What should you look for in good handmade chocolate? The texture, taste and smell of are all eseential in building towards that perfect, silky treat (and don't forget no added sugars/additives)!
